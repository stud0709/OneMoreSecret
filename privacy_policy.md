# OneMoreSecret: Privacy Policy

## Personal Data
OneMoreSecret (the "App") does not collect any personal data. No information whatsoever entered into the app is shared with the App developer or a third party.

## Internet Access
The App operates completely offline. 

## Data Storage
The App data is stored entirely on the device. 

## Camera Usage
Camera access is required to read QR code information.
